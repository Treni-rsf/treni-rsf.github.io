# 🚇 Treni

Welcome to **Treni** — a metro trivia game for anyone who loves maps, transit, and a good memory challenge.

How well do you *really* know your city's metro? Pick a network, choose a line, and travel it station by station — naming each stop 
along the way. It's the daily commute turned into a game.

## How to play

- **Pick a system and a line** — start with the networks you know best.
- **Name the stations** as you ride the line from one end to the other.
- **Close enough counts** — small typos, accents, and capitalization are forgiven, so you can focus on remembering, not spelling.
- **Stuck?** Use a hint to keep going.
- **Earn tickets** as you play and unlock more lines and cities.

Whether you're a daily commuter, a map enthusiast, or just competitive, there's always one more line to master.
